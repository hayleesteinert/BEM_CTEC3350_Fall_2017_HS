SWOT Analysis

Action Plan Action Plan
  • Emphasize credibility
  • Make website available to all clients
  • Use client and service knowledge to further fine tune the website and entice people to travel to it
  • Emphasize modern approach on website
  • Use blog feature to showcase new services, keeping both client and staff knowlegable
  • Use website to draw customers in who may not have originally been willing to travel

Strengths


Internal/Positive

Weaknesses


Internal/Negative
  • Credibility
  • Variety of customers of different ages
  • Have determined their clients and services
  • Possibly outdated practices due to older staff
  • No current website available
  • Unwilling to expand beyond 10 miles

Oppurtunities


External/Positive

Threats


External/Negative
  • Local business
  • Have been in the area for many years
  • Many new potential customers moving into the area
  • Located in a transitory area, new clients may perceive an older local business as antiquated
  • Opening of new clinics that will compete with them
  • Customers performing online shopping for pet gear instead of traveling to a buisness
Action Plan Action Plan
  • Will be able to use website to show ease of access to local customers
  • Use location and crediblity to entice new residents with pets to come to the buisness
  • Create excellent SEO capablity so that new residents will find the site easily when searching for a clinic
  • Create a modern and clean feel that matches the clinics theme
  • Create an easy method to schedule appointments and have access to communication with the clinic through the website
  • Creat an easy way to perform shopping for pet gear, and create methods that would entice the customer to come in and buy
